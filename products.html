Our Full Product Catalog

From grains to legumes, we offer a wide range of carefully selected and processed agricultural products. Everything your farm animals need, sourced from Venezuelan territory.

Grain

Corn (Maíz)

A highly nourishing food grain essential for livestock diets. Our corn is ideal for cattle, porcine, and poultry, providing the energy and carbohydrates that animals need to thrive. Sourced directly from Venezuelan agricultural zones.

Cereal

Rice (Arroz)

A premium cereal grain delivering excellent quantities of vitamins and minerals. Our rice products support the nutritional balance of livestock, promoting healthy growth and strong immune systems. Just what your animals need.

🐄 Livestock 💊 Vitamins & Minerals
Legume

Beans (Caraotas)

Rich in proteins, minerals, and fiber — an ideal complement for a healthy and sustainable livestock diet. Our beans are harvested in Venezuelan territory, providing a natural and affordable protein source for your farm animals.

💪 High Protein 🌿 Venezuelan
Grain

Sorghum (Sorgo)

A drought-resistant grain crop widely used in animal feed formulations. Sorghum provides high energy content and is an excellent alternative to corn in livestock diets. Packed with carbohydrates and essential nutrients for optimal animal growth.

🐄 Cattle ⚡ High Energy 🌾 Grain
Legume

Soy (Soja)

One of the world's most important protein-rich crops for animal nutrition. Soybean meal is a cornerstone ingredient in commercial animal feed, offering a complete amino acid profile that supports muscle development and overall health in farm animals.

💪 High Protein 🐔 Poultry 🐷 Porcine

Animal Feed (Alimento Balanceado)

Our balanced animal feed blends the best of our product catalog into a complete, nutritionally optimized formula. Designed specifically for farm animals, it combines grains, cereals, and legumes to meet the daily dietary requirements of your livestock.
